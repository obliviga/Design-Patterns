What is a Design Pattern?

A design pattern is a reusable solution that can be applied to commonly occurring problems in software design. Another way of looking at patterns are as templates for how you solve problems - ones which can be used in quite a few different situations. Patterns are not an exact solution. It’s important that we remember the role of a pattern is merely to provide us with a solution scheme. Patterns don’t solve all design problems nor do they replace good software designers, however, they do support them.
