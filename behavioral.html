What is a Behavioral Design Pattern?

Behavioral patterns focus on improving or streamlining the communication between disparate objects in a system.

An Interpreter pattern offers a scripting language that allows end-users to customize their solution.

A Builder separates the construction of a complex object from its representation so that the same construction process can create different representations. Construction details are hidden from the client entirely.
